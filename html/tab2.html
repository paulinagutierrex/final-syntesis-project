Special Relativity

What is it?

It is a scientific theory of the association between space and time, it deals with the movement of bodies in the absence of gravitational forces. Is a theory for description of the events that happen in the nature, that is, any phenomenon that is can be described by observers.
Was published in 1905 by Albert Einstein
What he basically postulates is that light travels through empty space at 300,000 kilometers per second and that nothing is capable of equaling that speed, much less exceeding it.
Where Maxwell's equations for electromagnetism were compatible with Newton's equations for motion.

Its postulates are:

1. Laws of physics are the same in all inertial reference frames
2. The speed of light in a vacuum is the same for all observers

Lorentz transformation

Are six parameters of linear transformations of a coordinate frame in space-time, relating the measurements of a physical quantity made by two different inertial observers
